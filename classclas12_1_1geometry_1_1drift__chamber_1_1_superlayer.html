Detailed Description

Superlayers of wire-planes contained within a single region.

This consists of several senselayers surrounded by guard layers.

the angles Region::thtilt() and thmin() are used in conjunction with Region::dist2tgt() to get the wire position midpoints:

Superlayer position determination
The wires in a given superlayer include guard, field and sense wires as indicated here:

Wires, wire planes and volumes
Warning
the guard layers (front and back) are stored in a single vector of Guardlayer objects. This only makes sense if there will always only be one in front and one in back. If this should change, consider splitting these into two vectors of Guardlayers maybe called front_guardlayers and back_guardlayers.
